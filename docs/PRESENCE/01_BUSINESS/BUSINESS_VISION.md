# Business Vision

**Mission:** Build India's AI-powered hyperlocal business operating system — connecting SMBs, customers, Google Business Profile, WhatsApp, Meta, and AI-driven attribution into one platform, distributed primarily through digital agencies.

**Tagline:** Own The Search. Own The Street.

## The Wedge (do not drift from this)
Not "GBP/WhatsApp/social management" (commoditized, near-zero WTP above ₹1,500–3,000/mo in India — free native tools cover 80% of that need). The real wedge: **AI-driven reputation-to-revenue attribution** SMBs cannot build themselves, combined with **WhatsApp-native automation depth**, sold primarily through an **agency white-label channel** rather than direct-to-SMB door-knocking.

## Why This, Why Now
- 7.83 crore MSMEs registered on Udyam as of Feb 2026 (govt. source) — real, growing, formalizing digital footprint
- WhatsApp Business API cost structure has just stabilized post the July-2025 per-message billing shift and Jan-2026 India rate adjustment — the cost-to-serve is now knowable and architectable around, not a moving target
- No competitor (Indian or global) has convincingly closed the loop from review/social/WhatsApp activity to measurable footfall/revenue — this is buildable, defensible, and compounds with data volume
- Founder-market fit: existing agency network, existing AI-marketing infrastructure (AgencyOS/Hermes), direct WhatsApp/CRM automation experience (Gallabox/Zoho at SKYi)

## 3-Year Ambition (average case, per financial model)
₹70–95 Cr ARR by end of Year 3, 8,000–10,000 paying businesses, ~45–50% of new ARR from the agency channel. Not a marketplace, not a directory, not a payments company — an attribution and automation layer that agencies deploy to their SMB books.

## What This Is Explicitly NOT (see COMPETITOR_ANALYSIS.md and BUSINESS_MODEL.md)
- Not a consumer discovery app/marketplace (Justdial/Magicpin's fight, not ours)
- Not a payments company (Podium's model works for them; opens PA/PG compliance surface we don't need pre-PMF)
- Not a B2B trade marketplace (IndiaMART/TradeIndia — non-competitive, non-relevant)
