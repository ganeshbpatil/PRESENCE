# Market Research (condensed from full diligence memo)

## Data Quality Warning
Hyperlocal/local-commerce "market size" reports from commercial research aggregators are frequently contradictory by orders of magnitude (found figures ranging from <$1B to >$140B for what's nominally the same India market, depending on undisclosed scope definitions). Do not cite a single market-research number in any external deck without checking it against this doc's sourced figures first.

## TAM/SAM/SOM (bottom-up, not market-report-derived)
- **TAM:** ~2.5–3 crore India consumer-facing local SMBs (theoretical ceiling, not addressable — do not use as-is in a pitch)
- **SAM:** 8–12 lakh businesses across Pune/Mumbai/Bangalore/Hyderabad/Delhi-NCR/Chennai with existing digital footprint + viable field/agency GTM reach
- **SOM (Year 3):** 8,000–15,000 paying businesses at blended ~₹8,000/mo ACV = ₹76–144 Cr ARR run-rate

## Adoption Tailwinds
MSME digital formalization accelerating (Udyam growth trend), no DLT/TRAI registration friction for WhatsApp API (unlike SMS), continued AI-inference cost decline favoring AI-credit gross margins over time.
