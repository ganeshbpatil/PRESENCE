# GTM Strategy

## Sequencing (per Pune area ranking + channel economics)

**Pilot zones (Months 4–7):** Baner, Koregaon Park — highest willingness-to-pay, easiest to sell, best for building the attribution case studies that fuel agency-channel sales later.

**Volume-scale zones (Months 7–12):** Kharadi, Viman Nagar, Wakad, Pimple Saudagar.

**Broadening zones (Year 2):** Kothrud, Aundh, Hadapsar.

**Expansion corridor (Year 2+):** PCMC/Nigdi, once the agency-channel motion is proven.

## Channel Mix Evolution
| Phase | Direct-to-SMB | Agency Channel |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 2 (pilot) | ~90% | ~10% |
| Phase 3 (scale) | ~50–60% | ~40–50% |
| Target steady-state | ~40% | ~50–60% |

## Agency Channel — The Primary Motion
Pre-sell to 3–5 agencies from the existing founder network (Phase 0.3) before building the white-label console. Formalize: self-serve agency onboarding, revenue-share structure, sales collateral built from real Phase 2 case studies — not before.

## Direct Sales Motion (secondary, but necessary for case studies)
Founder-led + one hire into pilot zones. Price at Starter/Growth only initially — no Scale tier until real multi-location reference accounts exist.

## What GTM Explicitly Does NOT Include (Year 1–2)
Paid consumer acquisition for a discovery app, marketplace-style two-sided growth tactics, discount/cashback-led acquisition (the Magicpin pattern this business is explicitly not replicating), franchise/reseller-beyond-agencies expansion.
