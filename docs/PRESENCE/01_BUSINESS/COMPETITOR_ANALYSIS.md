# Competitor Analysis (condensed)

## Category Correction
Three genuinely different categories get conflated in casual competitor lists: (1) directories/discovery (Justdial, Magicpin, Nearbuy, Yelp, Nextdoor), (2) B2B marketplaces (IndiaMART, TradeIndia — non-competitive, excluded), (3) reputation/CX/local-marketing-ops platforms (Birdeye, Podium, SOCi). PRESENCE competes in category 3, is adjacent to category 1, has zero overlap with category 2.

## India
| Player | Model | Sourced Metric | Exploitable Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| Justdial | Paid listing/campaign + directory | FY26 revenue ₹1,214 Cr (+6.3% YoY); 54.7M listings; but quarterly unique visitors **declined 4.7% YoY** | Consumer discovery relevance eroding even as SMB monetization grows — legacy directory losing to Google/social search; zero AI-native positioning |
| Magicpin/Nearbuy | Cashback/discount-led discovery | Not disclosed | Discount-subsidy-dependent discovery, doesn't build durable SMB software lock-in |
| Zoho Social/One | SaaS suite | Not disclosed | Feature, not outcome-oriented; no attribution layer; no WhatsApp-native depth |
| Vyapar/Khatabook | Bookkeeping-first | Large user base | Different job-to-be-done — potential integration partner, not competitor |
| Dukaan/DotPe | DIY storefront + QR/UPI | Not disclosed | Storefront-first, not discovery/reputation-first |
| ONDC | Govt open protocol | 900+ participants, 607+ cities | Not a product SMBs pay for directly — relevant as an integration/channel opportunity, not a threat |

## Global (direct category comps — validate the business model, not a threat to India GTM)
| Player | Model & Pricing | Scale (Sourced) | Learn/Exploit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Podium | $399–599/mo + $25–50/user + 2.15–2.9% payment fees | $12M ARR (2017) → $389M ARR (2023); $422M raised; $3B valuation, 11.2x revenue (2021) | Proves local-SMB SaaS can reach $3B at ~11x revenue with disciplined subscription+usage architecture — the playbook to emulate |
| Birdeye | Tiered subscription; launched "Agentic Marketing Platform" Jan 2026 | ~$100–150M+ ARR; 80,000+ customers; $93M raised | Confirms AI-agent repositioning already happening at the top of this category globally — "AI-powered" alone isn't differentiation anymore, execution depth is |
| Yelp/Nextdoor | Ad-supported consumer discovery | Mature US consumer bases | Consumer scale doesn't automatically convert to durable B2B SaaS revenue — cautionary, not a template |

## Feature Gap Matrix (condensed — not padded to 100 rows)
| Capability | Status |
|---|---|
| GBP+WhatsApp+Meta unified mgmt | GREEN — white space if WhatsApp-native depth is real |
| AI review-response automation | YELLOW — must build to parity fast, not a moat once built (Birdeye already ships this) |
