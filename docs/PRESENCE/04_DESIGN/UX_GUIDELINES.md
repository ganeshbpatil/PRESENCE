# UX Guidelines

- Draft-then-approve, never silent automation, for anything customer-facing (review responses, WhatsApp sends) in v1 — trust-building matters more than automation purity at this trust-building stage of the product's life
- Onboarding drop-off is a first-class metric from week 1 — measure per-step, not just overall completion
- Never force a blended view across business categories (F&B vs. salon vs. clinic) in any UI — segment-specific benchmarking/language where relevant, since owners think in their own category's terms, not "generic SMB"
- Attribution confidence should be visually honest — if `signal_completeness_pct` is low for a business, the UI should caveat the headline number rather than presenting false precision
