# UI Guidelines

## Screen Priority (ranked by business impact, not build ease)
1. **Attribution dashboard (P0)** — lead with a single "estimated revenue impact this month" headline, not a chart wall. SMB owners want the headline; agencies want the drill-down. A mediocre UI here undersells the actual moat.
2. **WhatsApp campaign builder (P0)** — must feel as fast as the native WhatsApp Business app or adoption dies. Template-first, not freeform-first.
3. **Agency multi-client console (P0)** — client-switcher is one click, not a navigation flow. Agencies manage 20–50 accounts; friction compounds.
4. **Review inbox (P1)** — draft-then-approve flow, never auto-send without explicit owner confirmation in v1.
5. **Onboarding/connect flow (P1)** — single guided flow, not three separate settings pages (known conversion killer in this category — test explicitly).
6. **Billing/credits (P2)** — standard SaaS pattern, don't over-invest design time here.

## General Principles
Mobile-first for the SMB-owner-facing views (many will check this on a phone between customers, not at a desk). Desktop-first acceptable for the agency console (agency staff work at a desk managing many accounts).
